Traditional Agroforestry Systems

Comprehensive research on sustainable farming models integrating trees with agricultural crops across India's rainfed ecosystems.

Sandalwood Research

Major contributor to sandalwood farming promotion across India with extensive research projects and training programs.

  • Multiple NMPB-funded research projects
  • Training program coordinator (2008-2018)
  • On-farm demonstration trials nationwide
NITI Aayog Member (2021)

Bamboo Conservation

Specialized research in conservation and cultivation of bamboo species with focus on sustainable development and edible applications.

  • Guadua angustifolia cultivation packages
  • Dendrocalamus stocksii & D. brandisii
  • Bambusa balcooa species promotion
  • Non-thorny bamboo species development
  • Edible bamboo shoots cultivation
